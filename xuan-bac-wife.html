Xuan Bac Wikipedia

Xuan Bac Wikipedia: - Xuan Bac was born on August 21, 1976 in Thanh Ha street, Thanh Mieu ward, Viet Tri city , Phu Tho province. He is a Vietnamese actor , comedian and television presenter. He began his career in 1995. He gained prominence for his part as Nui in the Vietnam Television comedy series Waves at the bottom of the river. He is also the lead actor in the political comedy shows Meet Weekend and Meet End of the Year.

Additionally, he serves as the host of the television game shows Vietnamese King of VTV3 and Chasing Pictures and Catching Words of Hanoi Radio and Television Station. Xuan Bac was given the title of Meritorious Artist in 2016 for his services to the arts. He was chosen to lead the Vietnam Drama Theater beginning in 2021, and in 2022 he was appointed chairman of the Vietnam Opera House.
