Who is Elizabeth Gillies?

Gillies was born as Elizabeth Egan Gillies on 26th July 1993, in Haworth, New Jersey. Born to her American parents, Lorrie Gillies,  and Dave Gillies, she is of White ethnicity and holds American nationality. She also has a younger brother named George Gillies.
