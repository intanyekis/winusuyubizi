How to Find the Perfect Wallet: A Buying Guide

Typically, a wallet is something that you don’t purchase often. Rather, it is ideal to have one high quality wallet that you use on a daily basis. Because a wallet is used so frequently, it’s important to invest in one that is durable, fashionable, and functional. To help you find a wallet that meets all these requirements, continue reading this buying guide to finding the greatest wallets.

What Are the Different Types of Wallets?

There are different styles of wallets available to suit many different needs. Some wallets have a large capacity for men who carry a lot of cards and cash. Others are more minimalistic for those who don’t carry many cards on a daily basis. No matter your preference, there is a style of wallet that will suit your needs.

Billfold wallet

This type of pouch comes in two basic versions — bifold and trifold. As the name suggests, a bifold wallet folds in half and looks classic, while the trifold has three sections and may be quite bulky.

Super slim wallet

Extra slim wallets allow you to keep mostly cards and little cash. The significant benefit of this non-bulky wallet is that it does not add much of an impression to your pocket. Hence, the wallet does not ruin your look. Additionally, the slim profile may help prevent discomfort when sitting down. Since these purses are made from sturdy frames, they maintain their shape much better than some classic styles.

Money clip wallets

These wallets are straightforward and are similar to a regular wallet. The only difference is that these wallets have a metal clip that helps to secure cash. A money clip wallet has the capacity to hold 15 to 25 bills at once. However, you should be careful not to overstuff the wallet, as the clip may become loose over time.

Which Factors Should You Look for When Buying a Wallet?

Color

If you want to be able to use your wallet on a day-to-day basis, be sure that it will match all of your outfits, choose a black or brown wallet. These colors are very versatile and will match any look.

Storage capacity

Depending on your requirements, you may choose a bifold or trifold wallet. A bifold wallet offers less storage than a trifold wallet. However, both of these serve the purpose of storing the daily essentials that a man needs. A bifold purse features one long pocket with several slots, which can be vertical or horizontal. You can use the open pocket for storing cash and the slots for keeping various cards. On the other hand, a trifold wallet also features a long open pocket with multiple slots. But these slots are typically oriented vertically. The open pocket is spacious enough for keeping cash, while the slots can fit money, cards, and receipts. Since the trifold wallet offers more space, it is usually bulkier than the former one. Because of this, if you carry it in your front pocket, it may be easily noticeable.

Slimness

Wallets have become a fashion statement for most men. The less space they occupy, the better they look. So, you should choose a wallet that does not have buttons or a lot of zippers, because these features will bulge out from your pockets and may look weird.

Longevity

If you want to purchase a wallet that will last you a long time, inspect the material that the wallet is made of. If it has poor quality, it will likely fade after a few uses. Moreover, harsh weather and daily use can wear down the wallet and may damage the essentials stored inside. Hence, you should invest in a product that is made from 100% genuine leather. However, the overall material is not enough to ensure the wallet will be durable. You should also examine the seams and make sure they are stitched well to ensure the durability of the wallet.

Anti-theft

Often overlooked, RFID blocking technology is important to prevent your card information from being scanned and stolen by thieves. Be sure to look for a wallet equipped with RFID blocking technology that blocks both major and minor data scanning frequencies.

How to Increase the Lifespan of a Wallet

Even if a wallet is made from premium quality material, it will wear down if you don't take care of it. The most common mistake that leads to the damage of a wallet is overstuffing it. Make sure to carry only what you need, because piling up your purse with bills will hurt the look and the quality of the leather.

What Are Some Things That You Should Not Keep in Your Wallet?

Wallets are meant for storing essential items like cards, IDs, cash, and slips. Day-to-day errands can result in overloading your purse with discount cards, library cards, social security cards, medical cards, too many coins, receipts, and more. If your pouch is stuffed with these things, take them out immediately and carry them only when you need them. Additionally, a wallet is not the best place to store keys.

People Also Asked

Are leather wallets better than wallets made of other materials?

A leather wallet is the most common choice because it not only looks stylish but also offers durability. However, not all leather wallets are made of 100% pure leather, so the quality may differ from expected. Wallets made of cowhide leather are the best, because this leather is made of tightly packed fibers, enhancing the durability.

Where should I keep my wallet?

You should keep your wallet in the front pocket of your pants or jacket. Avoid keeping it in your back pocket, because it can cause serious pain in your legs and back while sitting for a long time, due to the pressure it puts on the sciatic nerve.

Do I need a wallet?

If you don't like to carry a big bag to store your essentials, then you need a wallet to keep all of them organized and conveniently carry them around. Additionally, a wallet is an accessory that enhances your style.
