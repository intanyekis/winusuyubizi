Kate Mara is an American film, stage and television actress with an estimated net worth of $14 million.

Kate Mara has made her fortune from the roles that she has played in movies like Brokeback Mountain, We Are Marshall, and Shooter. The first time she performed in a film was in Random Hearts. Since then, Kate has performed in many movies. She has appeared in movies like Joe the King and Tadpole. She even starred in Urban Legends. Kate has worked on a television series on Fox too apart from many other roles in several TV shows. Her first television role was in the series Law & Order in 1997. Mara has made guest appearances in Madigan Men, and in many others.

Mara was born in Bedford, Westchester County, New York. Her father was Timothy Christopher Mara the vice-president of player evaluation for the New York Giants. Her Sister, Rooney Mara is an actress too. Her parental grandfather was a long time owner of the Giants.

Mara lives in Los Angeles. She's married to Jamie Bell with whom she has a daughter. She is a fan of NFL teams “Giants” and “Steelers”. Mara began acting when she was just nine when she was a part of the school musical. She has attended several youth art schools and has appeared in many school plays. Kate studied musical theatre from the Tisch School of the Arts at New York University. She did an audition for a television drama but did not get that role. She was determined to become an actor after this rejection. We can say that she has certainly been successful.
