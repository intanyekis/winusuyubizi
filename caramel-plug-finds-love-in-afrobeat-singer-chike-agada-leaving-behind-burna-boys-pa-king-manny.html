Caramel Plug finds love in afrobeat singer Chike Agada, leaving behind Burna Boy's PA King Manny

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Caramel Plug has moved on from the drama surrounding her previous relationship with Burna Boy’s PA, King Manny, and is now romantically involved with afrobeat singer Chike Agada.

Caramel Plug’s relationship with King Manny was tumultuous, with reports of infidelity and public altercations. A viral video showing them in a heated argument and near physical altercation seemed to be the final straw that led Caramel to end the relationship due to constant disrespect.

Rumors about Caramel Plug’s new relationship with Chike Agada began circulating when she posted a picture of flowers she received from him. Chike Agada further fueled the rumors by reposting the picture on his own social media. Neither of them has explicitly denied the relationship rumors.

Recently, Caramel Plug shared a cute photo on her Instagram story, holding a bag with a caption that mentioned “@iamchikeagada” and expressed her love for the bag.

Caramel receives N1m from Burna Boy’s PA as birthday gift

In May, NewsNow Nigeria News reported that Instagram comedienne Ogechi Ukonu, popularly known as Caramel Plugg, had a special birthday celebration. Her then-boyfriend, King Manny, gifted her a substantial sum of N1 million. Additionally, Caramel Plug received some dollars directly from Burna Boy, adding to the excitement of her day.
