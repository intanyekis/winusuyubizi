Joe Alwyn's Tattoo Status Is Nearly As Mysterious As Whether Taylor Swift Is Secretly Inked

June 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • Fans believe Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn have secret tattoos.
  • Unlike other A-list stars, Alwyn and Swift don't publicize whether or not they are inked.
  • Taylor Swift alluded to her relationship with Joe Alwyn in her new album multiple times.

Even before Taylor Swift released her 11th album "The Tortured Poets Department," fans knew there would be some songs about Joe Alwyn. Swifties are clever detectives who analyze social media pages and interviews for clues and Easter eggs, and when she puts out new music, they waste no time trying to guess who each song is about. Even if "Getaway Car" from "Reputation" isn't a song about Tom Hiddleston, it's fun to imagine that it could be.

There's no doubt that several tunes off "Tortured Poets" will be added to the list of the singer's most popular songs . After Taylor Swift released her music video for her first single "Fortnight," people did even more detective work. Fans thought Swift and Alwyn were engaged because she had a garter on in one scene. They also noticed something else: Swift had tattoos in the video. Swift's "Fortnight" collaborator Post Malone has countless tattoos and in one scene, Post Malone's tattoos get transferred to Swift's face, which is a fun image. This made everyone curious about Swift's tattoo status.

In this article, we'll take a look at the mystery surrounding whether Joe Alwyn and Taylor Swift have tattoos, along with a closer look at their six-year relationship.

Do Joe Alwyn And Taylor Swift Have Tattoos?

Joe Alwyn and Taylor Swift's tattoo status is a mystery because neither singer has said they have tattoos.

According to Extra TV, there is no indication that Alwyn is tattooed. When Swift sang the lyrics "Like a tattooed golden retriever" in her song "The Tortured Poets Department," she might have been referencing Matty Healy, not Alwyn.

It's possible that Joe Alwyn has secret tattoos that don't show in any photos and that he doesn't talk about. After all, Alwyn wanted his Swift romance to stay "private," and he might have the same approach to other parts of his life.

Based on her comments, it doesn't seem like Swift is interested in tattoos. She sounded disgusted at the idea of getting ink on her body. However, that interview took place in 2012 when she was 23 years old, and as of April 2024, she is 34. She might have changed her mind and gotten a tattoo... but she might not want her fans to know about it.

It's interesting to note that Taylor Swift has gotten fake tattoos for music videos in the past. It seems that her "Fortnight" tattoo was a temporary one, and in 2021, Swift had a back tattoo in her video for "ME!" While fans consider this Swift's worst song, the video was cool. But just like the "Fortnight" video made fans wonder if Swift had a secret tattoo, it was hard not to think that the "ME!" video tattoo was real.

Inside Taylor Swift And Joe Alwyn's Relationship

While Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn's tattoo status may be mysterious, so was their six-year relationship. Although fans know the timeframe of their time together, it was always hard to get a real feel for what their romance was like because neither star talked about it at the time.

According to People, The Sun published an article about Swift and Alwyn dating in May 2017. While they might have started dating a bit before then, no one is sure of the exact date. As time passed, the two became more serious but kept their relationship secretive. While they went on vacations and even attended award shows together, they never seemed to want people to know much about their romance.

Alwyn and Swift split up in April 2023 and a source told People that Alwyn had a hard time:

"With Taylor's level of fame and the attention from the public."
