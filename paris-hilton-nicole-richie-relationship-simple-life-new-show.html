Paris Hilton And Nicole Richie Have An Explosive Relationship History

Since Paris Hilton and Nicole Richie were kids, they have rubbed elbows with the rich and famous thanks to who their parents are. For that reason, there is no doubt that most people wouldn't have understood Richie and Hilton's childhoods, which would have bonded them.

With that in mind, it makes sense that Hilton and Richie have been friends since they were kids. That years-long friendship was on full display when Richie and Hilton starred in The Simple Life during the 2000s.

Sadly for Hilton, Richie, their fans, and the people in their lives, however, the former co-stars and friends had a huge falling out. In May 2024, The U.S. Sun updated its timeline of Richie and Hilton's relationship, which cites several sources. According to that timeline, Hilton and Richie's relationship began falling apart in 2005.

According to the timeline, Hilton first admitted that she and Richie were no longer friends as part of a 2005 statement she released. However, Hilton wouldn't outline what caused their falling out.

“It’s no big secret that Nicole and I are no longer friends. Nicole knows what she did, and that’s all I’m ever going to say about it.”

Around the same time, Richie told Today's anchors that she and Hilton "just grew apart". Richie also stated the gossip around the friendship ending “just turned into a much bigger thing than it is".

While Richie tried to downplay the drama, The U.S. Sun's timeline states that rumors point to her as the one who got the ball rolling. According to the rumors, Richie showed Hilton's infamous intimate tape during a party. When Hilton found out, she was furious and that led to their longstanding friendship ending if the rumors are right.

On the bright side, that drama eventually subsided and Richie and Hilton revealed that they'd made peace. During this era, neither woman pretended they were best friends, but they spoke affectionately about each other.

According to the timeline, E! News quoted Richie describing the state of her relationship with Hilton in 2014.

"Paris is somebody that's been in my life since day one. I haven't spoken to her in a while technically, but if you really want to get into it, my view of a friendship is somebody that you don't necessarily have to talk to every day, somebody that you can call when you need them and they're just going to be there."

The timeline also reveals that Hilton talked about how much Richie means to her during a 2019 Watch What Happens Live appearance. "I love Nicole. We were actually texting three days ago because it's our high school reunion, so she was reminding me of the date."

In 2021, Richie attended Hilton's marriage to Carter Reum where they were pictured reuniting. Then in 2024, it was revealed that Hilton and Richie were making big plans together.

Paris Hilton And Nicole Richie Are Returning For A New Streaming Show

In May 2024, Paris Hilton and Nicole Richie's social media followers were surprised by a collage. The collage, which featured photos of Richie and Hilton together when they were young, was uploaded to both stars' Instagram accounts simultaneously.

When both former reality TV stars uploaded the collage to their account, their fans didn't know that it seemed to be a harbinger of things to come. After all, Hilton and Richie have been on good terms, so the duo celebrating their history doesn't seem that remarkable.

However, around the same time the collage appeared on Instagram, TMZ published a bombshell report. According to that article, Richie and Hilton were preparing to star in a new show together decades after The Simple Life ended.
