Chris Jericho Presenting Live Talk Is Jericho Podcast w/ Special Guest Christian The Day Of SummerSl

September 2024 · 2 minute read

The morning of Summerslam the place to be is Caroline’s on Broadway in Times Square!

Be there as Chris Jericho and Jay “Christian” Reso face off in an intimate conversation in a live episode of the Talk is Jericho podcast. Be there as they pull back the curtain on their wrestling careers to tell stories that have never been told.

​Don’t miss your chance to hear directly from these icons of sports entertainment.

Following the podcast there will be a Q&A session moderated by Wrestlezone, 120 Sports & Bischoff on Wrestling’s Nick Hausman. He will put the mic in the fans’ hands to ask the questions that they’re dying to know the answers to.

​Each ticket entitles entry to this once in a lifetime event, a photo with each of Chris Jericho and Jay Reso (2 photos), plus an autograph from each superstar.

Additional single Signature tickets will be sold separately for $30.

VIP PRE EVENT BRUNCH $249.99  FOR ONLY 25 PEOPLE:  JOIN CHRIS AND JAY FOR AN INTIMATE BRUNCH BEFORE THE EVENT, ASK THEM ALL THE QUESTIONS YOU WISH, TAKE PHOTOS WITH THEM BOTH AND GET 4 AUTOGRAPHS FROM BOTH SUPERSTARS.

​Following the event guests that are attending Summerslam can easily hop on the 2/3 or A/C trains to take the 20 minute subway ride directly to Barclays Center.

This event is replacing the previously announced “Adam & Jay LIVE” event that had been previously announced featuring former WWE World Heavyweight Champions Edge and Christian. Adam was recently cast in the TV show Vikings and due to his new filming schedule is no longer able to attend the event. Also, due to Chris Jericho’s busy SummerSlam schedule the event had to be moved up one hour to 11 am instead of Noon. Tickets that were purchased for that event will be honored for this special live edition of Talk Is Jericho. Any fans that would like refunds because of the talent and time change can respond to their confirmation e-mail with the word “Refund”.

This event should be the perfect compliment to any fans in town for SummerSlam. Can’t wait to see everyone there!
