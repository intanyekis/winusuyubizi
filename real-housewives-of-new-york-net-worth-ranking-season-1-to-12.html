Real Housewives Of New York: Net Worth Ranking For Every Wife From Season 1 To Now

August 2024 · 6 minute read

The ladies on the Real Housewives reality franchise definitely spend a lot of money on clothing, vacations, and fancy dinners out. It's easy for us to assume that they have super high net worths and that their hefty bank accounts allow them to lead these fabulous lifestyles.

There may be reality TV stars who fake their wealth, but that's not the case with the cast members of The Real Housewives of New York. Even people who only appeared for a single season have accumulated quite a lot of wealth over the years. While some own successful businesses and others just married up, almost every member of the cast has found a way to hit the million-dollar mark. Curious about the net worths of these famous women? Keep reading to find out.

16 Jules Wainstein Has The Lowest Net Worth With $1.5 Million

Jules Wainstein has a $1.5 net worth, which is the lowest of any cast member who has appeared on the show.

We've probably heard about Jules Wainstein's arrest and we definitely remember that her marriage didn't seem peachy keen, at least from what we observed in any scenes of them together.

15 Alex McCord's Only Got $2 Million

Alex McCord's got a $2 million net worth. That seems pretty low when we think about it, especially since when Alex and her hubby Simon appeared in the early seasons of the show, they were throwing some serious money around. They would always go shopping and if the bill was thousands or even tens of thousands of dollars, they really didn't care at all.

14 Newcomer Leah McSweeney Has $2.4 Million

According to Distractify, Leah McSweeney has a net worth of $2.4 million.

We don't know a ton about Leah yet since she's new to the show and this is her first season, but we do know that she has an adorable daughter named Kiki and the two of them do everything together. We're looking forward to see how Leah fares this season.

13 Former Housewife Cindy Barshop's Net Worth Is $6 Million

Cindy Barshop isn't a super familiar face for RHONY fans as she isn't on the show anymore, but she was in the fourth season, and we might remember her from back then.

Cindy has a net worth of $6 million, which might not be as high as some others, but hey, we would be totally fine with having this much cash in the bank.

12 RHONY Staple Sonja Morgan Has $8 Million

Celebrity Net Worth puts Sonja Morgan's net worth at $8 million. This is about what many fans would expect since she always mentions her various businesses on the show, and we know that she recently sold her townhouse and made some money from that. She has also been on the series from the beginning, so she's been earning a regular salary.

11 Heather Thomson, Who Owned A Shapewear Line, Has $10 Million

Heather Thomson owned a shapewear line back in the day and that's something that she often talked about on the show.

How much money does Heather have? Celebrity Net Worth says that it's $10 million for the former housewife.

10 It's $18 Million For Pinot Grigio Lover Ramona Singer

Finance.yahoo.com puts Ramona Singer's net worth at $18 million. We all love Ramona, whether she's begging for pinot grigio (the only wine that she'll drink) or fighting with the other ladies for the millionth time.

9 Kelly Bensimon Is Up Next With A $20 Million Net Worth

Celebrity Net Worth says that Kelly's net worth is $20 million. She made a big impression on the show, especially when she was rude to Bethenny and said that she was better than her (ugh).

8 Aviva Drescher Also Has $20 Million In The Bank

Celebrity Net Worth says that Aviva Drescher's net worth is $20 million. We all remember this former housewife because of that one time she took her prosthetic leg off and threw it.

It's interesting to hear that Aviva is one of the wealthier women who have appeared on The Real Housewives Of New York.

7 Kristen Taekman, Who Was On Seasons 6 And 7, Has $20 Million

Not everything on the Real Housewives is true, but Kristen Taekman does live a fabulous and wealthy lifestyle. According to Celebrity Net Worth, she's also got $20 million.

According to Reality Blurb, Kristen's husband Josh was part of the Ashley Madison controversy, which we might recall hearing about.

6 Fan Fave Dorinda Medley's Got A $20 Million Net Worth

Celebrity Net Worth says that Dorinda Medley also has a net worth of $20 million. She always talks about how she wants to "make it nice" whether she's invited the women over to her home in the Berkshires for Halloween, Christmas, or in the summer. And we love watching all the dramatic scenes that are filmed at her fabulous place.

5 Cabaret Fan LuAnn De Lesseps Has $25 Million

In recent seasons of RHONY, Luann has become totally obsessed with cabaret (and some of the other ladies haven't had the nicest things to say about it).

It looks like this cabaret fan has some real money in the bank, though, so it's totally working out for her: according to Celebrity Net Worth, Luann's net worth is $25 million.

4 Bethenny's Ex-BFF Jill Zarin's Net Worth Is $35 Million

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Jill Zarin has a net worth of $35 million. She was on the show after her husband Bobby passed away and she and Bethenny Frankel actually spoke to each other.

But before that scene, Jill was known as Bethenny's ex-BFF since the two had a massive falling out. It was a huge deal for the Bravo show.

3 Socialite Tinsley Mortimer Has $35 Million

Celebrity Net Worth says that Tinsley Mortimer's net worth is $35 million.

This is definitely a lot of money and we're not super shocked to hear it. After all, Tinsley was introduced to fans as a socialite who has a lot of family money and who can afford to live in a hotel year-round.

2 Writer Carole Radziwill Is Doing Super Well With A $50 Million Net Worth

Celebrity Net Worth says that Carole's net worth is $50 million. She's actually the second wealthiest woman to appear on the Real Housewives of New York. Carole is a successful author with many published books from memoir to fiction.

1 Bethenny Frankel's A Super Successful Businesswoman And Has $70 Million

We might have guessed that Bethenny Frankel has the highest net worth of any cast member on RHONY and that's definitely the truth.

Celebrity Net Worth says that she has $70 million, which isn't surprising. She's the woman who started the Skinnygirl company, after all.
